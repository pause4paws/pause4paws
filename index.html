Help & FAQ

Everything you need to know about Pause4Paws

Getting started
What is Pause4Paws? ▾
Pause4Paws is a community-powered map for Dubai residents to report missing pets, log sightings, and help reunite animals with their owners. It covers Damac Hills 2, Town Square, Mira, Serena, Villanova, Arabian Ranches 2, Tilal Al Ghaf, Dubai Hills Estate, Remraam, and many other nearby communities.
Do I need an account to use the app? ▾
You can browse the map and see the coloured pins, but you cannot tap a pin to see details, use AI matching, or log anything. To use any feature you need a paid lifetime membership — 38 AED, once, forever.
How much does it cost? Is it really lifetime? ▾
Yes — completely lifetime. A single sign-up fee of 38 AED — that's just £8, €8, and less than $10 — for life! No renewals, no price increases, no subscriptions. Ever. Pay once and you're a member forever.
How do I pay? ▾
After creating your account, a Wio QR code is shown on screen. Open your banking app (any UAE bank), go to "Scan & Pay" or QR payments, scan the code, and confirm the 38 AED. Your account will be activated once payment is received.
Reporting a missing pet
How do I report my missing pet? ▾
Tap the red Report Missing Pet button. Sign in (or create an account), fill in your pet's details — name, species, breed, colour, distinguishing features, microchip number, the date and time they went missing, and which cluster or street. Add at least one photo. Your pet appears as a red pin on the map immediately. An immediate WhatsApp notification is sent to everyone in your area who has signed up for alerts — including your pet's photo and your contact details so neighbours can help straight away.
My pet has been found — how do I update the report? ▾
Open your pet's report, tap Full details, then tap Mark as Back Home. The pin turns yellow and your community's reunited counter increments.
Found or spotted an animal
I found an animal — what do I do? ▾
Tap I Found a Pet. This enters sighting mode — drag the map to the exact location you found the animal and tap Confirm. You can link the sighting to a specific missing pet or submit it as a general sighting. Use Narrow search to filter by what you see and check if any missing pet matches.
What are Known Strays (blue pins)? ▾
Known Strays are animals that community members or cat feeders have identified as regular strays in the area. Listing them prevents them being repeatedly reported as "found" pets. Cat feeders who know their local strays are encouraged to add them here.

What if I find an injured animal outside hours? ▾
Log the injured animal as a sighting on Pause4Paws with GPS coordinates. If you are able, take the animal to the nearest vet. If not, in the description when logging the sighting, state that the animal needs urgent help and request support from the community. Keep the animal calm and in a quiet, contained space — do not offer food or water before a vet assessment.
Featured pets
What is a Featured Pet? ▾
Featured pets are showcased on the home screen, marked with a purple pin on the map. Every subscriber receives an immediate WhatsApp when your pet is featured. Every morning at 6am all subscribers receive a featured missing pets WhatsApp — maximum daily exposure. Every evening at 6pm a second WhatsApp is sent. Feature your pet for 25 AED per week — use your pet's name as the payment reference. Cancel any time.
Communities & map
Which communities are on the map? ▾
Currently: Damac Hills 2 (all clusters with real GPS), Damac Hills, Mudon, Mudon Views, Arabian Ranches 1, 2 & 3, Town Square, Mira, Mira Oasis, Serena, Villanova, Tilal Al Ghaf, Dubai Hills Estate, Remraam, The Sustainable City, Layan, Al Waha, Majan, Liwan, Motor City, Dubai Sports City, Cherry Gardens, and Al Barsha South. More can be added — use the request box below.
A cluster or area is in the wrong place — how do I report it? ▾
Use the Map Correction box below. Select the community, describe what's wrong or missing, and — if you can — paste the correct GPS coordinates from Google Maps (right-click any spot on Google Maps to copy the coordinates). Your report goes straight to the admin who will update the map.
How do I get GPS coordinates from Google Maps? ▾
On a computer: open Google Maps, find the exact spot, right-click on it, and the coordinates appear at the top of the menu — click them to copy.

On a phone: open Google Maps, long-press on the exact spot until a red pin drops, then tap the coordinates shown at the bottom of the screen to copy them.

Paste them into the Map Correction box below.

📱 Install Pause4Paws on your phone
How do I add Pause4Paws to my home screen? ▾
You can install Pause4Paws as an app on your phone — it will appear on your home screen just like any other app, opens full screen, and works exactly like a downloaded app. It's free and takes 30 seconds.

📱 On iPhone (Safari):
1. Open https://pause4paws.github.io/pause4paws/ in Safari
2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen (the box with an arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add" in the top right
5. The Pause4Paws icon appears on your home screen ✅

🤖 On Android (Chrome):
1. Open https://pause4paws.github.io/pause4paws/ in Chrome
2. Tap the three dots ··· in the top right corner
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app"
4. Tap "Add"
5. The Pause4Paws icon appears on your home screen ✅

Once installed, just tap the icon to open the app — no browser needed.

📷 Uploading photos
What type of photos can I upload? ▾
Any photo taken on a phone will work. Accepted formats include:

✅ JPG / JPEG (standard photos)
✅ PNG
✅ HEIC (iPhone default)
✅ WebP

Photos should be under 10MB for fastest upload. Standard phone photos are always fine. Screenshots work too.
